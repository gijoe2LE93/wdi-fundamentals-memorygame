Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

10 Benefits to playing memory games Playing memory games regularly brings the following benefits:

  • Healthy exercise to your brain.
  • Improve concentration.
  • Enhance the cognitive skills.
  • Boost the brain functionality.
  • Train your visual memory.
  • Increase short-term memory.
  • Prevent memory-related illness.
  • Help to keep focus.
  • Increase attention.
  • Playing memory games against the clock forces us to think fast.
